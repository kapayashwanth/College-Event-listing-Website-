About Cultural Club

The Cultural Club of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Nagrecoil Campus, is dedicated to promoting and nurturing cultural activities among students. We organize various events throughout the year to showcase the diverse talents of our students in music, dance, drama, and other cultural activities.

About FOSS Club

The FOSS (Free and Open Source Software) Club is a student-run organization that promotes the use of open-source software and technologies. We conduct workshops, hackathons, and coding competitions to encourage students to contribute to the open-source community.
