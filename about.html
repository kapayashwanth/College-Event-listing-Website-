About Amritostav

Our Vision

Amritostav envisions creating a platform where innovation meets celebration, fostering a spirit of unity and creativity among students from diverse backgrounds. We aim to be the premier cultural and technical fest that showcases the best of student talent and innovation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to organize an annual fest that brings together students from various disciplines to participate in academic, cultural, and technical events. We strive to create an environment that encourages learning, collaboration, and the exchange of ideas while celebrating our rich cultural heritage.

Our Values

We believe in fostering innovation, promoting cultural diversity, and creating memorable experiences for all participants. Our values include excellence, inclusivity, creativity, and the celebration of both traditional and modern forms of expression.

Our History

Amritostav has been a cornerstone event at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham since its inception. Over the years, it has grown into a prestigious platform that attracts participants from across the country, showcasing the best of student talent in various fields.

Our Campus

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Nagrecoil Campus, provides the perfect backdrop for Amritostav. With state-of-the-art facilities and a vibrant student community, our campus is equipped to host a wide range of events and activities.

Our Team

The Cultural and FOSS Clubs of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham work together to organize Amritostav. Our dedicated team of students and faculty members ensures that every aspect of the fest is meticulously planned and executed.
